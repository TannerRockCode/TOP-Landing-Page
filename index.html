Welcome to the Outdoor Adventure Club

We are a group of outdoors enthusiasts that recognize the importance of community and our connection to nature. We encourage you to join our club to be part of the fun in the great outdoors!

Our Favorite Activities

Hiking and camping in more remote areas allow us to get away from digital media and enjoy the beauty of mountains, lakes, and sunsets.

Explore while surfing the asphalt on a longboard. Longboarding gives you freedom to travel many miles to visit parks and city sites.

We love Pickleball with its simple rules and age friendly game play. Enjoy the competitive weekly pickleball meetups!

Volleyball is a club favorite during the summer, particularly sand volleyball at parks and reservoirs.
